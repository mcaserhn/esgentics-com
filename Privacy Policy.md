## 1. Introduction

ESGentics Pte. Ltd. ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. By using our Site or services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

- **Contact Information**: Name, business email, company name, phone number, job title (when you submit inquiry forms)
- **Business Information**: Project requirements, region of operation, ESG compliance needs
- **Communication Records**: Correspondence via email, contact forms, or scheduled calls

- **Technical Data**: IP address (anonymized), browser type, device information, operating system

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell** your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: Trusted vendors who assist with hosting (AWS Singapore/Azure Europe), analytics (Google), or communication (email services). All processors are bound by data protection agreements.
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by Singapore law, court order, or regulatory authority with jurisdiction over our operations.
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with prior notice where legally required).
- **With Your Consent**: For any purpose explicitly authorized by you.

**Geographic Restriction**: All data processing occurs in Singapore, European Economic Area (EEA), or North America. **No data is transferred to or processed in mainland China.**

## 5. Data Sovereignty & Regional Compliance

### 5.1 Data Residency Guarantee
You may specify your preferred data residency region during engagement. We will configure our infrastructure to ensure:
- **EU Clients**: Data processed/stored in EEA (GDPR compliant)
- **North America Clients**: Data processed/stored in US/Canada (CCPA/PIPEDA aligned)
- **Middle East/Southeast Asia Clients**: Data processed/stored in Singapore or regional AWS/Azure zones

### 5.2 Cross-Border Transfer Safeguards
Where data must cross borders (e.g., Singapore HQ accessing EU project data), we implement:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by EU Commission
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3+) and at rest (AES-256)
- Access controls with role-based permissions and audit logs

- **Training**: Staff trained on data protection principles and incident response
- **Certifications**: Working towards ISO 27001 certification (target: 2025)
